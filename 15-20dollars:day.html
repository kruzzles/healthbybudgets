The first meal is a lentil soup with spicy sausage


-Add two tablespoons of olive oil and one pound of fully cooked sausage (thats cut into cubes)


-Cook sausage until browned


-When the sausage is ready tranfer it to a a pot


-Add 3 cups onions, 1 3/4 cup carrots, 1 1/4 one cup celery and 1/2 teaspoon of itailian seasoning


-Cook the mixture until the onions are translucent


-Set the heat to low and let sit until the flavours blend


-Season with salt and pepper


Ready to serve!


Total cost: $6.07




The second meal is poor mans stew


-Add 1lb of ground beef to a skillet and cook until browned


-Add the beef to a slow cooker with 1.5lbs of potatoes, 1 diced onion and 3 sliced carrots


-In a seperate bowl whisk together 6oz can of tomatoe paste, 2 cups water, 1/4 tsp pepper, one tsp onion powder and one tsp dried oregano


-Pour everything into a slow cooker


-Stir the mixture


-Cook for 7-8 hours


Ready to Serve!


Total cost: $6.24




The third meal is tortellini pasta salad


-In a large bot cook half a box of tortellini


-While the pasta is cooking cut up a bell pepper and a cucumber


-Add the cooked pasta and the vegetables, mix together


-Add 1/4 cup vinaigrette dressing


-Refrigerate for at least two hours


Ready to serve!


Total cost: $5.85
